# Product Vision

## Core promise

Generate blog articles automatically for any website.

## Positioning

Autoblog Agent is not a generic AI writer. It is a website-first SEO blog automation platform that understands a business before it generates content.

## Target users

- Small businesses
- Consultants
- Service businesses
- Niche website owners
- Agencies
- SaaS companies
- Bloggers

## Why it matters

Most AI writing products begin with prompts. Autoblog Agent begins with the website, the niche, the existing content footprint, and the operational workflow required to publish continuously.

## Product workflow

1. User adds a website manually.
2. System crawls and analyzes the website.
3. System infers the niche, business focus, content pillars, and current content gaps.
4. System runs a basic SEO and content audit.
5. System discovers article opportunities relevant to that niche.
6. System converts opportunities into article plans.
7. System generates publication-ready drafts automatically.
8. User reviews, edits if needed, and exports or publishes.

## V1 principles

- Manual website onboarding
- Website-first understanding
- Automation with clear workflow states
- Human review before publishing
- Monetizable without enterprise overhead
- Clean SaaS operator experience instead of chat-first UX

## V1 scope

- Dashboard
- Website management
- Website analysis
- SEO audit
- Content opportunities
- Content plans
- Draft studio
- Automation runs
- Export center
- Product landing page

## Deliberately out of scope for V1

- Full billing implementation
- Complex team management
- Advanced permissions
- CMS-specific publishing integrations
- Enterprise analytics or approval chains
